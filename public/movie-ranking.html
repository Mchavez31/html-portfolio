The Best Movies According to Michael

My top 3 movies of all-time

Interstellar

This is my favorite space and exploration movie. I like the interesting images, creative planet environments, and the complex science related concepts.

Avatar

This is my favorite scifi movie. I like all the creativity used in depicting a new world with unkown living beings and animals as well as the idea of humans being able to transfer their consciousness into an avatar.

Avengers End Game

This is my favorite action/superhero movie. I love all the teamwork between the superheros and all the creative fighting scenes in the movie. It also has funny scenes and a very good storyline. Some of the story is so good that it can get emotional at times.
